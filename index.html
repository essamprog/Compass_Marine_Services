About Us

"Compass Marine Services is a Jeddah-based marine supply and ship chandling company serving all major Saudi Arabian ports, including Jeddah Islamic Port, King Abdullah Port, and Yanbu. We provide a full range of ship provisions, bonded stores, spare parts, safety equipment, and technical marine support. Our team ensures fast delivery, reliable service, and competitive pricing 24/7 to meet all vessel requirements."

Specialist Marine Services

Our experts have a deep maritime heritage and strong supplier relationships, specializing in ship supply, whether it's materials, goods, or food. From technical stores and safety equipment to fresh provisions and bonded stores, we ensure vessels receive the highest-quality products. Backed by our 24/7 global team, we guarantee fast delivery and strict quality control every step of the way.

Sustainable Shipping, Transparent Operations

Your cargo deserves more than just safe delivery; it deserves a journey powered by responsibility. We embrace the latest advancements in eco-friendly shipping, from fuel-efficient vessels and green ports to innovative digital solutions that ensure full control and complete transparency. By combining operational excellence with environmental stewardship, we move your cargo while protecting our oceans for generations to come.
